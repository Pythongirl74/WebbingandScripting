Python

Project: Face Mask Detector (2020)

In 2020, I embarked on an innovative project that combined my passion for technology and public health: a Face Mask Detector using Python. In the midst of the global pandemic, I saw an opportunity to contribute to public safety. Leveraging Python's powerful libraries like OpenCV and TensorFlow, I developed a sophisticated system capable of real-time face detection and mask recognition. The application utilized deep learning algorithms to accurately identify whether a person was wearing a face mask or not. This project not only honed my programming skills but also reinforced the importance of technology in addressing real-world challenges, making a positive impact on our community during a critical time.
